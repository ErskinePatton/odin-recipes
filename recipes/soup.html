Miso Soup


A delicious and healthy soup for those cold winter days

Perfect alongside any entree


Ingredients

  • Water
  • Dashi Granules
  • Miso paste
  • Diced tofu
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Add Dashi Granules and Miso paste to water
  2. Bring water to a boil
  3. Add tofu and green onions
  4. Reduce heat and enjoy

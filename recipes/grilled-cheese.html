Grilled Cheese

Description

A quick and yummy lunch


Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Softened Butter or Olive Oil
  • Lunch meat, sliced onions, or any other filling you desire

Steps

  1. Cover one side of each slice of bread with the butter or olive oil
  2. Place the sliced cheese and other filling onto the unbuttered/unoiled side of the bread
  3. Place the other slice of bread on top of the filling to complete the sandwich
  4. Place the sandwich onto a hot pan or grill and cook until cheese is melted and bread is browned
